Recipes Straight From The Garden

GRILLED SQUASH

Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients: 4 medium sized squash, 1/2 cup olive oil, salt, pepper, and thyme.

Instructions: 1. Preheat the grill to medium heat. 2.Cut squash horizontally into at least 1/2 inch thick pieces. 3. Brush olive oil onto squash and season. 4. Grill squash for 7-8 mins per side depending on desired tenderness. Serve.

GRILLED CORN

Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients: 5 stocks of corn, 1/2 cup melted butter, salt, and pepper.

Instructions: 1. Preheat the grill to medium heat. 2. Peel back the husks of the corn to the base and remove the threads. 3. Brush the corn with melted butter and season. Cover the corn with the husks. 4. Grill for 15 mins and rotate occasionally. Serve.

SAUTEED GREEN BEANS

Cook Time: 6 mins

Ingredients: handful of green beans, 2 cloves of garlic, 1/4 cup olive oil, salt, and pepper

Instructions: 1. Bring a large pan of water to boil. 2. Cook for 4 mins. Then drain and rinse under cold water. 3. Heat oil over medium heat in skillet. 4. Add beans and garlic for two mins. Add seasoning when stirring beans. Serve.

LETTUCE SALAD

Cook Time: 3 mins

Ingredients: lettuce, shallots, lemon, parmesan, olive oil, salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions: 1. Take desired amount of lettuce and place in a large bowl. 2. Add 2 thinly sliced shallots, red pepper flakes, and a cup of grated parmesan to the bowl. 3. Add dressing(salt, pepper, olive oil, lemon juice) to salad bowl and toss. Serve.
